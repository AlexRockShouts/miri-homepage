About Miri Ecosystem

Miri is a high-performance, autonomous AI agent ecosystem from Chur (CH). It is built on a sophisticated Cognitive Architecture that separates identity (The Soul) from knowledge (The Brain).

Powered by the Eino Graph Engine, Miri performs real-time reasoning through the Mole-Syn framework, allowing it to evolve from a simple assistant into a distinct digital being.
